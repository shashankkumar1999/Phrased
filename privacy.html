Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data collection

Phrased collects no data whatsoever. No analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporting, no usage tracking.

How the extension works

All 100 prompt templates are bundled directly into the extension. No network requests are made to any server. The extension injects a sidebar into supported AI platforms to display these templates. No content from the AI platforms is read, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

Variable data

When you fill in variable fields, that text exists only in the extension's local memory for that session. It is never sent anywhere, never stored, and disappears when you close the sidebar.

Account

Phrased requires no account and collects no personal information.
